This is an image that i drew over an epic scene from a show. I tried to use the original scene to make a poster but the image was not high resolution so I had to make my own. My interest in making posters has forced me to start making my own images to use in my posters. The process of making this image includes drawing all the lines, coloring it, then adding the highights and shading.
